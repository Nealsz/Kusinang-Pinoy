Pancit Palabok

Ingredients

  • 250g rice noodles (bihon)
  • 1/2 cup pork belly, boiled and sliced
  • 1/2 cup shrimp, boiled and peeled
  • 2 cups shrimp stock
  • 2 tablespoons annatto powder (achuete)
  • 4 tablespoons fish sauce (patis)
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch, dissolved in water
  • 2 cups crushed chicharrón (pork cracklings)
  • 3 hard-boiled eggs, sliced
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • Lemon or calamansi wedges

Steps

  1. Boil the rice noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, heat the annatto powder with shrimp stock until it simmers. Strain and set aside.
  3. In a separate pan, heat oil and sauté garlic until golden brown.
  4. Add the sliced pork belly and shrimp. Cook until shrimp turns pink.
  5. Pour the annatto-infused shrimp stock into the pan.
  6. Season with fish sauce and let it simmer for a few minutes.
  7. Add the dissolved cornstarch to thicken the sauce.
  8. Arrange the boiled rice noodles on a serving plate.
  9. Pour the shrimp and pork mixture over the noodles.
  10. Top with crushed chicharrón, sliced hard-boiled eggs, chopped green onions, and fresh cilantro.
  11. Serve with lemon or calamansi wedges on the side.
  12. Enjoy your delicious Pancit Palabok!
