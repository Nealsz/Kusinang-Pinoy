Pork Adobo

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 head garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine the pork, soy sauce, and garlic. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Saut√© the onion until translucent.
  3. Add the marinated pork, including the marinade, to the pan. Cook until the pork is browned on all sides.
  4. Add the vinegar, bay leaves, peppercorns, and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for about 1 hour, or until the pork is tender.
  5. Season with salt to taste. Continue to simmer until the sauce has reduced to your desired consistency.
  6. Serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy your Pork Adobo!
