Bicol Express

Ingredients

  • 500g pork belly, sliced thinly
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 cup coconut cream
  • 1 cup shrimp paste (bagoong)
  • 10-15 pieces red chili peppers (siling labuyo), chopped
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, sliced thinly
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Saut√© garlic, onion, and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add the sliced pork belly and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Stir in the shrimp paste (bagoong) and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Pour the coconut milk into the pan and bring to a simmer.
  5. Add the chopped red chili peppers (siling labuyo) and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Simmer for about 20-25 minutes until the pork is tender and the sauce thickens.
  7. Pour the coconut cream into the pan and stir well.
  8. Let it simmer for another 5 minutes.
  9. Adjust seasoning if needed.
  10. Serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy your spicy and creamy Bicol Express!
