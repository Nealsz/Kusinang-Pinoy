Chicken Tinola

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2-3 pieces green papaya (or sayote), cut into wedges
  • 2 cups malunggay leaves (or spinach)
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce (patis)
  • 4-5 cups water
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a pot, saut√© ginger, garlic, and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Pour water into the pot and bring to a boil.
  4. Lower the heat and let it simmer until the chicken is tender.
  5. Add green papaya (or sayote) and cook until tender.
  6. Season with fish sauce, salt, and pepper.
  7. Add malunggay leaves (or spinach) and simmer for a few more minutes.
  8. Remove from heat and serve hot. Enjoy your Chicken Tinola!
