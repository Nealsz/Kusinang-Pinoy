Chicken Inasal

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken pieces (leg quarters or thighs)
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1/4 cup calamansi juice (or lemon juice)
  • 1/4 cup Sprite or 7-Up
  • 1 head garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, grated
  • 1 stalk lemongrass, pounded
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup annatto oil (achiote oil)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine vinegar, calamansi juice, Sprite, minced garlic, grated ginger, pounded lemongrass, brown sugar, ground black pepper, and salt to make the marinade.
  2. Add chicken pieces to the marinade, making sure they are well coated. Marinate in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours, preferably overnight.
  3. Prepare the grill for medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the marinated chicken pieces onto skewers, if desired.
  5. Grill the chicken over medium-high heat, turning occasionally and basting with annatto oil, until cooked through and nicely charred, about 20-25 minutes.
  6. Remove the chicken from the grill and serve hot with steamed rice and a dipping sauce made from vinegar, soy sauce, calamansi juice, and minced garlic. Enjoy your Chicken Inasal!
