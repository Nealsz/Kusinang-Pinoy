Kare-Kare

Ingredients

  • 1 kg oxtail, cut into 2-inch lengths
  • 1/2 kg beef tripe, cleaned and cut into strips
  • 2 bundles string beans (sitaw), cut into 2-inch lengths
  • 2 pieces eggplant, sliced
  • 1 bunch baby bok choy (pechay)
  • 1 banana blossom (puso ng saging), sliced
  • 3 tablespoons annatto seeds (achuete)
  • 2 cups ground peanuts
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 4 tablespoons shrimp paste (bagoong)
  • 4 cups water
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Boil oxtail and beef tripe in water until tender. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a separate pan, saut√© garlic and onion until fragrant.
  3. Add annatto seeds and ground peanuts. Stir well.
  4. Pour water into the pan and let it boil. Simmer until the sauce thickens.
  5. Add peanut butter and shrimp paste. Mix until well combined.
  6. Put the boiled oxtail and beef tripe into the sauce. Cook for a few minutes.
  7. Add string beans, eggplant, banana blossom, and baby bok choy. Simmer until vegetables are cooked.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Remove from heat and serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy your Kare-Kare!
