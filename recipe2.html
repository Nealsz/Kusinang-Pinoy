Sinigang na Baboy

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly, cut into cubes
  • 1 large onion, quartered
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 radish, sliced
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 1 bundle kangkong (water spinach), separated
  • 2-3 pieces green chili peppers (siling haba)
  • 2 liters water
  • 1 packet sinigang mix
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. In a large pot, bring water to a boil.
  2. Add the pork belly and cook until tender, about 30-45 minutes.
  3. Add onions, tomatoes, and radish. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Stir in the sinigang mix until dissolved.
  5. Add the eggplant, kangkong, and green chili peppers. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  6. Season with salt to taste.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy your Sinigang na Baboy!
