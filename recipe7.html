Lumpiang Shanghai

Ingredients

  • 500g ground pork
  • 1 cup carrots, minced
  • 1 cup onion, minced
  • 1 cup green onions, chopped
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Lumpia wrappers
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix together ground pork, carrots, onions, green onions, eggs, bread crumbs, soy sauce, ground black pepper, and salt until well combined.
  2. Place a spoonful of the mixture in the center of a lumpia wrapper.
  3. Roll the wrapper tightly around the filling, folding the sides inward as you roll.
  4. Seal the edge with water to secure the lumpia.
  5. Repeat with the remaining mixture and wrappers.
  6. In a deep pan, heat oil over medium heat.
  7. Fry the lumpia in batches until golden brown and crispy, about 3-4 minutes per batch.
  8. Remove from the oil and drain on paper towels.
  9. Serve hot with sweet and sour sauce or banana ketchup for dipping. Enjoy your Lumpiang Shanghai!
